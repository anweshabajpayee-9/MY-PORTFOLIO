Predicting Hereditary Sickle Cell Anaemia Using Deep Learning and Chromatography

My research advances Sickle Cell Anemia (SCA) detection and classification using deep learning, achieving 96% accuracy with MobileNet. While I explore the potential of integrating MobileNet with Arduino for a compact healthcare device in the future, our current focus is on enhancing classification with HPLC reports. This research supports the National SCA Elimination Mission 2047 by enabling automated blood sample analysis for early detection, aligning with the mission's goals of screening and early intervention. Additionally, our platform will offer resources for understanding SCA causes and prevention, aiding in treatment and education.
